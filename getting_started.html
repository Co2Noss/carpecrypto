Mac Installer Instructions

Find the Terminal Application on your Dock.

Open your applications folder then your utilities folder and locate Terminal. Double click to open and then run the following command by copying and pasting. 

curl -o installer.sh https://carpecrypto.me/installer/installer.sh && bash installer.sh
